What are mental disorders?

Mental illnesses are health conditions that affect the way a person behaves, thinks, or feels. Even if the symptoms shown are light, any mental disorder can change a person’s everyday life by adding stress. While it isn’t completely clear why mental disorders are developed, studies show that a combination of biological, psychological, and environmental factors can cause a mental illness to erupt.
